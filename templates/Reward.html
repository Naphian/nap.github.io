National Ethiopia Examination (8th and 10th grade)

My educational journey began with a noteworthy performance in the National Ethiopia Examination during 10th grade, where I secured Straight A's in a range of subjects. These included Amharic, English, Mathematics, Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Civic Education, and History. The document showcasing these achievements will be attached, but more than just a piece of paper, these grades were my first stepping stones in the world of rigorous academic commitment.
